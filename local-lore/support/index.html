Safety and access

Public-source coordinates, ownership, hours, and access rules can change. Local Lore does not grant permission to enter any location. Stay on public roads and designated paths; never trespass or enter private property, restricted land, closed buildings, tunnels, ruins, or unsafe structures.

Folklore and reported encounters

Haunted, UFO, cryptid, and other unexplained accounts are presented as reported lore for cultural interest and entertainment—not as verified fact. Corrections and better historical sources are welcome.

Privacy requests
