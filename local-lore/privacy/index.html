Local Lore

Privacy Policy

Effective July 23, 2026

The short version: Local Lore requires no account and contains no advertising, purchases, or behavioral analytics. TripperDeeLabs does not receive, sell, or store your device location.

Who publishes Local Lore

Local Lore is published by TripperDeeLabs. Privacy questions, deletion requests concerning submitted email, and corrections may be sent to feedback@tripperdeelabs.com.

Location and nearby discovery

When you request nearby lore, Local Lore obtains your device location and sends coordinates directly to public information services—including Wikimedia projects such as Wikidata, OpenStreetMap Overpass services, iNaturalist, and National Register geographic services—to retrieve nearby stories, wildlife observations, and historic sites.

TripperDeeLabs does not operate an intermediary location server and does not receive your coordinates. These public providers process requests under their own privacy policies.

Local Lore uses location only while the app is open and actively discovering. It does not request background-location permission, run a location foreground service, monitor movement, or perform scheduled discovery after the app is closed.

Address, town, and ZIP/postal-code search

You can search for another location instead of granting current-location access. Search text may be processed by the Android device's configured geocoding provider. Local Lore does not save the entered address or send it to TripperDeeLabs. The resulting coordinates are used with the public discovery services described above.

Information stored on your device

Your Favorites, settings, and recently retrieved area cache are stored in Local Lore's private app storage. Android may include eligible app data in device-transfer or encrypted-backup systems according to your device and Google account settings. Clearing Local Lore's storage or uninstalling it removes the local copy unless Android later restores an eligible backup.

Maps, sources, translation, and sharing

The Lore Map loads Leaflet code and OpenStreetMap tiles from public services. Opening directions, an original source, or translation transfers you to the selected external provider, whose privacy policy applies. Local Lore does not receive your map, browser, translation, or sharing activity.

Suggestions and corrections

The suggestion feature opens your external email application with a template. Nothing is sent automatically. If you choose to send the message, the information you include is delivered through your email provider to TripperDeeLabs and used to review the suggestion, investigate a safety or accuracy concern, or respond to you. Suggestions are never published automatically.

Children

Local Lore is a general-audience travel and education app. It is not designed as a child-directed service and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If Local Lore's data practices materially change, this page and its effective date will be updated before the corresponding app release.
